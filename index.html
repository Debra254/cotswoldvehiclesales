About Us

A Family-Run Dealership You Can Trust

Cotswold Vehicle Sales is a small family business situated in Eastington on the edge of the Cotswolds, serving Stonehouse and the surrounding area. With over 30 years of automotive industry experience, we're passionate about delivering excellent customer service in a no-pressure environment, making your car buying experience smooth, stress-free, and enjoyable.

Repeat customers and glowing 5★ reviews are a reflection of the care and commitment we bring to every sale.

Our team's wealth of experience means we understand what matters most to buyers — quality, reliability, and honesty. Whether you're browsing or ready to buy, we are here to help you find the right vehicle to suit your needs and your budget.
