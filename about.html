About Us

Your trusted local dealership

Over 30 Years of Excellence

Cotswold Vehicle Sales is a small family business situated in the village of Eastington on the edge of the Cotswolds. With over 30 years of automotive industry experience, we are passionate about customer service and creating a no-pressure sales environment.

We help our customers find vehicles that meet their needs and budget. Our strong base of repeat customers and 5-star reviews reflect our commitment to honest, reliable service.

Every vehicle is hand-picked, road tested for a minimum of 50 miles, workshop inspected, and supplied with a fresh service and minimum 3-month warranty. Extended warranty options of 6, 12, or 24 months are also available.
